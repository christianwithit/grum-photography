Get To Know Me

Hello, I'm Grum

My Story

A Passion for Authentic Moments

My love for photography began long before I ever photographed a wedding, but the first time I captured two people saying "I do," I knew I'd found my calling. Today, I specialize in documenting weddings across Uganda — from Kampala to Jinja, Lake Victoria to the Rwenzori foothills — creating images that feel natural, authentic, and full of life.

More than beautiful photos, I want you to remember how it felt to be there — surrounded by love, joy, and the people who matter most.

My Philosophy

Every Wedding is a
Unique Story

I believe wedding photography is about more than posed portraits — it's about telling the story of your day in a way that feels genuine to you. From the big, emotional moments to the quiet in-between details, my goal is to document it all with care. With a balance of gentle guidance and unobtrusive storytelling, I'll help you feel comfortable while creating timeless images you'll treasure for years to come.
